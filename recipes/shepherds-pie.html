Shepherds pie

Description

Follow this recipe to make an amazing shepherds pie in just a few easy steps.

Ingredients

  • one pound of ground pork
  • onion (diced)
  • Canned tomatoes
  • sugar
  • lasagna pasta sheets
  • cheese
  • eggs
  • herbs & spices

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
